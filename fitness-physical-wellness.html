Fitness and Physical Wellness

The Cancer Support Center offers a variety of physical activities that can reduce pain and inflammation caused by treatment side-
effects. Reduce stress and anxiety through gentle activity and gain breathing techniques. Get in shape with other participants who
might have similar cancer related physical side effects.

Cancer and its treatment alters our bodies, often causing us to reduce our physical activity to avoid harming our bodies or causing
increased pain. At The Cancer Support Center, we have specifically designed programs that gently provide fitness and physical
activities that will heal your ease your body back into shape.
