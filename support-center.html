Testimonial

"When my Dad got cancer, it was a weird feeling. I cannot really explain it. I try to keep happy
thoughts when I am sad. Talkng with Jason (my counselor at The Center) and seeing Champ

-Ian, a 10-year-old-participant

A cancer diagnosis impacts the entire family. The Cancer Support Center is here so that no one walks the cancer journey alone.
With your support, we provide programs to children, families and adults to help them cope with the distress, anxiety and emotions
that come with a diagnosis.

You can provide counseling sessions for individuals like Ian who need help navigating through this uncertain time in their lives. You
make it possible for pet therapy dogs like Champ to visit The Center as well as other stress management programs like art therapy,
storytelling, guided imagery and massage. You allow women, men and children to participate in fitness classes, nutrition programs,
support groups and educational workshops specifically designed for anyone with cancer and their loved ones.

There is still time to make a difference this year. Your donation will provide programs and services to thousands of individuals with
cancer through programs focused on counseling/stress management, fitness/physical wellness, nutrition, education, and body
image.

The Center's service hours have increased more than 30% this year as compared to 2014. We need your help to serve this growing need.
