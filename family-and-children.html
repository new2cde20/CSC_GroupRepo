Family and Children

When a loved one is diagnosed with cancer, children, teens and family members may experience anxiety, sadness, fear, and
confusion. The Center is here to help minimize distress and create an environment for the family to relax and take comfort.
The Family and Children program has the following options available to the parents, the children or the family unit. Please look at the options below:

  • Parent or child individual therapy
  • Art therapy
  • Family counseling
  • Family Fun Nights
  • Kids Club
  • Children and Teen Groups/Workshops
  • Nutrition consultations (entire family)

For more information on these programs, please see our newsletter. If there are other activities or programs
you'd like The Center to provide to support your family or a family you know, please let us know by calling The Center at:
+1 (708) 798-9171. We are here to provide hope, help, and healing - for you and your family. Let's walk this journey together!
