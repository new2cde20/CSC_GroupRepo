Honor or Memorial Gifts

Consider making a gift to The Cancer Support Center in honor or in memory of someone special in your life. Tribute gifts can be made in honor of a special occasion, to wish someone a speedy recovery, to celebrate survivorship, or to say thank you for a kind deed. Memorial gifts can provide a meaningful way to remember a loved one or friend who has passed away.

Please contact Jen Linares to make any memorial or honor gifts, jlinares@cancersupportcenter.org

Matching Gifts

Maximize your contribution by taking advantage of your employer’s matching gift program. Ask your human resources department for a matching gift form, fill out your portion, and enclose it with your donation.

Monthly Giving Program

Join our monthly giving program and have your donation spread out over time. Support us by providing pre-arranged gifts of a set amount through your credit card. Through the Monthly Giving Program, you will provide reliable and predictable revenue to help us with offering programs and services to people affected by cancer in our community.

Why join?

Efficient: Monthly giving is an incredibly efficient and effective way to support The Center.

Affordable: You will be spreading your contribution throughout the year. This can be helpful for budgeting. And it means you can plan in advance how much you want to contribute to help our program participants.

Convenient: Once you’ve decided how much you want to give, no further action is required. Your donation will appear on your credit card statement each month. And at the beginning of each year, for tax purposes, The Center will send you a single receipt for all gifts received during the preceding calendar year.

Feels good: You will feel good that you are supporting The Center monthly and not have to worry about remembering to make that donation!

We hope you will consider becoming a Monthly Giver today. Know that when you join us, you can change your monthly pledge amount or cancel at any time. For more information contact The Center today.

Workplace Giving

If your employer engages in a workplace giving campaign, you may be able to designate your payroll-deducted donations to The Cancer Support Center. The Cancer Support Center is a qualified 501(c)(3) organization, all donations are 100% tax-deductible.

Shopping Options

Great shopping opportunities from Amazon.com and iGive.com that benefit The Cancer Support Center. Both sites are FREE to use, easy to set-up, and what’s best: They donate a percentage of your purchase amount to our free programs!
